\textbf{Term / Acronym} & \textbf{Description} \\ \hline

RCO & Feedback-Coupled Cryptographic Observation Protocol \\

FCAS & Feedback-Coupled Agentic System \\

PoR & Proof of Reflexion; cryptographic mechanism binding telemetry to originating computational state \\

MVR & Minimal Viable RCO; baseline configuration ensuring core integrity guarantees \\

SRE & Site Reliability Engineering; operational discipline for maintaining system reliability and performance \\

TSS & Threshold Signature Scheme; cryptographic method requiring multiple parties to generate a valid signature \\

MPC & Multi-Party Computation; distributed cryptographic technique for joint computation without revealing private inputs \\

SHA3-256 & Cryptographic hash function based on the Keccak algorithm, producing a 256-bit output \\

Merkle-Chain & Sequential cryptographic structure linking data blocks via hash dependencies \\

Bencode & Deterministic serialization format with lexicographical ordering and length-prefix encoding \\

Canonical Serialization & Encoding process ensuring identical binary output for identical logical data \\

Fixed-Precision Scaling & Transformation of floating-point values into integer representations using a predefined scaling factor \\

Zero-Trust & Security model where no component is inherently trusted and all actions require verification \\

Telemetry & Structured runtime data generated during system execution \\

Telemetry Batch & Grouped unit of telemetry processed and verified as a single entity \\

Ingestion & Process of receiving, validating, and storing telemetry data \\

Lineage & Ordered sequence of telemetry batches linked cryptographically \\

Integrity Proof & Cryptographic evidence verifying authenticity and correctness of telemetry \\

Auditor Node & Independent verification entity participating in validation and signature generation \\

Agent Node & Computational entity generating telemetry data \\

Sidecar & Auxiliary component operating alongside a primary system to provide additional functionality \\

gRPC & High-performance remote procedure call framework used for communication between distributed components \\

io\_uring & Linux kernel interface for asynchronous I/O operations with reduced syscall overhead \\

AVX-512 & CPU instruction set enabling parallel computation on 512-bit data registers \\

TPM & Trusted Platform Module; hardware-based security component for key storage and attestation \\

PCR & Platform Configuration Register; TPM register used for system state attestation \\

TDA & Topological Data Analysis; method for analyzing structural properties of data \\

Lyapunov Stability & Mathematical concept used to determine stability of dynamic systems \\

Deterministic State & System condition yielding identical outputs for identical inputs across executions \\

Immutable Ledger & Storage structure where data cannot be altered without detection \\

Replay Attack & Reuse of previously valid data to produce unintended system effects \\

\end{longtable}
